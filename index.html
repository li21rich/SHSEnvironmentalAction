Introduction

We are a passionate team of youth environmental activists, primarily from Saratoga High School. One of our focuses are environmentally impactful bills, such as SB499, AB1423, and SB676.

MISSION STATEMENT


Our club’s mission is to advocate for positive environmental change, both at the local and state level.

Our goal is to create a

  • Safer
  • Healthier
  • Environmentally Friendlier
home for both residents of Saratoga and California as a whole.
